ABOUT THE FEST

WHERE TECH
MEETS
ADRENALINE

XTASY is the premier annual techno-cultural festival — a high-voltage collision of innovation, creativity, and raw competitive energy. Hosted by the brightest minds on campus, XTASY brings together thousands of students, industry leaders, and visionaries across three electrifying days.

From bleeding-edge workshops and hackathons to pulse-pounding robotics battles and live proshow concerts — this is where legends are made.
